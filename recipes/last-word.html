The Last Word

This drink already looks so good it does not need garnish

The Last Word is my personal favorite cocktail. It has it all! It is a little sweet, herbal, tangy and got a kick to it. it has the potential to be drank as an aperativo or as a digestivo.I also really would not mind one at the end of the night! the only thing about this drink I dont like all too much is the fact that because of the acidity you get incredible heartbuirn after only 3 glasses. Though let me tell you that after three glasses you probably had enough anyway.

Ingredients

  • 20ml Gin
  • 20ml Luxardo
  • 20ml Chartreuse vert
  • 20ml Lime juice

Discription

  1. Put some ice into a martini glass
  2. Add the ingredients into the small part of the shaker tin
  3. Fill up the large side of the shaker tin and empty the now chilled martini glass
  4. Shake it vigourasly untill ice starts forming on the outside of the tin
  5. Open the tin, and with the use of an hawthorn strainer and a finestrainer you pour the drink into the martini glass
  6. I do not see the need for garnish here but if you must, a lime zest
